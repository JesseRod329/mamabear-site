Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 25, 2026

MamaBear ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the MamaBear mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Account information: Name and email address used to create your profile.
  • Baby and child data: Names, birth dates, feeding logs, nap records, diaper changes, pumping sessions, growth measurements, and milestone tracking.
  • Cycle tracking data: Menstrual cycle dates, symptoms, and flow information.
  • Family data: Chore assignments, grocery lists, meal plans, school events, and family member names.
  • Health data: If you choose to enable HealthKit integration, we read and write weight, height, and sleep data to Apple Health.

Automatically collected information:

  • Purchase information: Subscription status managed through Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not process or store payment details.
  • Device information: Basic device type for compatibility purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and operate the App's features (baby tracking, cycle tracking, chores, groceries, school hub, meal planning).
  • Generate insights, predictions, and trends based on your logged data.
  • Sync health data with Apple HealthKit when you grant permission.
  • Enable family sharing features between invited family members.
  • Process and manage your subscription through Apple's StoreKit.
  • Send you notifications you have opted into (feeding reminders, chore reminders, school events).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and, when family sharing is enabled, synchronized securely. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption. Health data accessed through HealthKit is subject to Apple's additional privacy protections and is never transmitted to external servers.

4. HealthKit Data

MamaBear integrates with Apple HealthKit to read and write health data including body weight, height, and sleep analysis. We access this data only with your explicit permission.

  • HealthKit data is used solely to display health information within the App and sync your tracking data.
  • We never sell, share, or use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • HealthKit data is never transmitted to third parties.
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in your device's Settings > Health > Data Access.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Family members: Data you choose to share with invited family members through the App's sharing features.
  • Apple: Subscription and purchase transactions are processed by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

6. Third-Party Services

MamaBear does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

7. Children's Privacy

MamaBear is designed for parents to track their children's data. The App is not intended for use by children under 13. We require parental consent (COPPA compliance) before any child-related data is entered, and all such data is controlled by the parent or guardian who creates the account.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored within the App.
  • Export your data using the PDF export feature (available with MamaBear+).
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time through the App's settings.
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions through your device settings.
  • Withdraw notification permissions at any time.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed. Subscription records managed by Apple are subject to Apple's retention policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App and updating the effective date above.
